Geb Buns
video here (slightly modified)
A recipie for pretzel buns that are soft and not knotted.
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1.25 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3.125 cups flour
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 4.5 cups water
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • some coarse grain salt
Instructions
  1. Put water, brown sugar, and 1 tbsp butter (half) in a bowl & mix. Ensure butter is liquid and water is warm. You want the temperature of the liquid to just barely NOT burn your finger.
  2. Add yeast to the liquid. Add salt after.
  3. Add all flour. Mix in 1 cup at a time.
  4. Knead for 15-30 mins.
  5. Put dough in bowl, then add olive oil. Coat the dough ball with the oil.
  6. Leave the dough in a warm place (100-140 F) for 1-2 hours, or until doubled.
  7. Cut dough into 12 pieces, then form balls by pinching.
  8. Fill a pot with 4.5 cups of water and 1 tsp baking soda, then bring to a boil.
  9. Put buns in the pot 3 at a time for 30s on each side.
  10. For each bun, cut a hole on the top using scissors, add a small amount of melted butter, then sprinkle coarse salt.
  11. Bake in an oven for 20 minutes at 400 F.
